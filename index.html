Why use Monotone?

Monotone is the most cutting-edge social media app you've ever seen. With some of the most useful and reliable functions, Monotone is a place where you can simply chill.

Share your life.

Life goes through many phases. The most common phases are happiness and sadness. Make people's lives hopeful by sharing your happiness. You never know what makes a troubled person happier! If you're sad, share your feeelings on Monotone. Out of the millions in the world, someone could help you out.

Ask a question.

Do you have a question? With the people using Monotone, someone can answer that question. But wait. Everyone has knowledge about certain fields, don't they? Well, if you see someone who's asking a question about something you know about, go ahead and answer them.

Limit your time.

We know that everyone needs some time to just relax and rewind, but a bit too much of relaxation can result in something called "waste-of-time". No one likes that. We thought it would be a good idea to show you how much time you spent on Monotone, so that you can keep track of the time that's passing. Without a doubt, everyone knows that time flies when someone's have fun.
